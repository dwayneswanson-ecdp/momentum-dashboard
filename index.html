            What To Say

            "My current total compensation is €43K. Based on my research and the scope of this role, I'm targeting a base of €65-70K."
            "I'm targeting €65-70K base, which reflects market rate for this profile and what I've seen for AE roles in Paris."
            "The jump reflects a category change from hardware/telecom cycles to SaaS, and that I was underpaid relative to my output. I'm confident in the value I bring."

            Red Flags In A Role

            • Ramp period over 6 months — signals a slow sales motion. Ask directly.
            • No clear OTE formula — means variable is discretionary, not formula-based. Avoid.
            • French-only meetings — contradicts the English-first goal. Ask in the interview.
            • No equity at scale-up — fine, but worth asking. Some Series B/C offer BSPCE.

            Questions To Ask Them

            • Sales cycle length — first contact to close for a mid-market deal? Target: under 6 months.
            • Quota attainment — % of AEs who hit quota last year? Target: above 60%.
            • Inbound vs outbound — higher inbound means stronger PMF.
            • Commission structure — fixed formula is safer than one that varies by deal type.
            • Promotion path — what does progression from AE look like?
